        The Swap Engine automates accumulation: donations from large holders stream into the swap wallet and a smart contract executes one swap every ~12 hours to build tBTC. These donations are locked and released on Streamflow, enabling a steady Dollar-Cost Averaging plan into tBTC via DEX routes like Jupiter.

        All accumulated tBTC flows into the Vault, a decentralized and verifiable treasury on-chain. Holders can see the balances and participate in governance directly in the tBTC treasury on Realms.

        The Burn Engine is fueled by the Torrino DAO community: trading profits are used to buy back Nummus and send them to the burn address, permanently reducing supply. Join the DAO and contribute to the burn strategy here: Torrino DAO Hub.
